Our Services

Comprehensive Diagnostic Testing

From routine blood work to advanced molecular diagnostics — all under one roof with expert validation.

Biochemistry Tests

Glucose, Lipid Profile, Liver & Kidney Function, Thyroid, Vitamin B12, HbA1c, and more comprehensive panels.

Hematology & CBC

Complete Blood Count, ESR, peripheral smear, coagulation studies, and more — powered by Sysmex XN-330.

Cancer Marker Tests

Tumor markers including CA-125, PSA, CEA, and AFP for early detection and monitoring of malignancies.

Hormone Assays

FSH, LH, TSH, T3, T4, Prolactin, Testosterone, and other hormonal evaluations for endocrine assessment.

Urine & Body Fluids

Routine urine analysis, cerebrospinal fluid, pleural fluid, and other body fluid examinations.

Genetics & Molecular Dx

DNA analysis, genetic testing, and molecular diagnostics for accurate disease profiling and personalized medicine.

ECG Testing

Electrocardiogram for comprehensive cardiac evaluation — detecting arrhythmias, heart conditions, and more.

Uroflowmetry

Non-invasive urodynamic testing to evaluate urinary flow rate and diagnose bladder or prostate conditions.

Histopathology & Cytology

Tissue biopsy examination and cytological studies for accurate diagnosis of lesions and abnormalities.
